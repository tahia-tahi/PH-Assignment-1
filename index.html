Bangladesh 2.0

Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Intelligent infrastructure, renewable energy, technology-powered education, the rise of fintech, and eco-friendly agriculture.

Sustainability

Clean energy, sustainable industries, eco-conscious urban planning, regenerative agriculture, and environmental preservation

Infrastructure

Intelligent urban centers, advanced transportation systems, durable housing solutions, seamless digital connectivity, and streamlined logistics

Empowerment

Tech empowerment, women-driven businesses, equitable policies, workforce upskilling, and youth-driven innovation.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

In Bangladesh 2.0, the path to progress is built on empowerment for all. Imagine a future where every person, regardless of gender or background, can pursue their dreams without obstacles. Women, who are the pillars of our economy, will drive this transformation. We will ensure that they have access to education, technology, and financial resources. While microfinance started the journey, it's time to take it a step further by fostering a culture of micro-entrepreneurship in every corner of the nation.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
